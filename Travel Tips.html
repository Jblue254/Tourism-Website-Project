Maasai Mara, Kenya

4 star rating worldwide

  • Clothing with neutral colors
  • Comfortable closed-toe shoes
    • Read More

Paris, France

8 Star Rating Worldwide
  • A slightly dressier pair of shoes (optional): For evenings out
  • Sunglasses: Essential for sunny days
  • Lightweight, breathable fabrics: Cotton, linen, rayon.

Statue of Liberty, U.S.A

8 star Rating Worldwide
  • Pre-booked ferry tickets
  • Printed or digital copies: Have them readily available.
  • Photo ID: For security checks and age verification if applicable

Magra, India

8 star Rating Worldwide
  • Pre-booked tickets: Highly recommended to save time in queues.
  • Passport (for foreigners) or valid government-issued ID (for Indian nationals): Essential for entry and verification, especially if you booked online
  • Only a small bag is allowed. The smaller, the better, to speed up security checks.

London, England

4 star Rating Worldwide
  • A light jacket or trench coat: For cooler evenings or sudden rain showers.
  • Extremely comfortable walking shoes: This is paramount!
  • Sunglasses.
  • SunScreen

Rome, Italy

8 Star Rating Worldwide
  • Comfortable Walking Shoes
  • Sun Protection: The Colosseum and Roman Forum are largely open-air with very little shade.
  • Small Bag/Backpack: Large bags, suitcases, and big backpacks are not allowed inside the Colosseum, and there's no locker or storage facility

Bejing, China

8 Star Rating Worldwide
  • Comfortable Walking/Hiking Shoes: This is non-negotiable
  • Water: Hydration is paramount, especially in summer. Carry at least 1.5-2 liters per person.
  • Valid Passport & Visa (if applicable): Always carry your ID
  • Small Towel or Handkerchief: For wiping away sweat.

Sydney, Australia

3 star Rating Worldwide
  • Arrive Early: Allow ample time to pick up tickets (if not digital), check your coat/bag, find your seats, and perhaps grab a drink.
  • Smart Casual Attire: While there's no strict dress code, people generally dress up a bit for a performance. Avoid very casual wear like shorts, flip-flops, or beachwear.
  • ID/Payment Method: Standard essentials for travel.

Rome, Italy

2 Star Rating Worldwide
  • Strict Dress Code Compliant Clothing: This is the most crucial item. You will be denied entry if you don't adhere.
  • Water Bottle (empty): You cannot bring food or drinks into the Basilica.
  • Valid ID: Your passport or national ID might be requested
  • Portable Charger/Power Bank: For your phone, as you'll likely be taking many photos outside and using navigation/audio guides.
